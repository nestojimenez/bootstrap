Automation and Process Improvement

In order to improve processes thru the use of the Automation a standard steps have been defined, this optimize the use of resources for the project .

Step 1: Understand

Automation team should understand the need by reviewing CTQs, Anual Volume, Product Design, Estimated Layout.

Step 2: Assess

Automation team will review project feasability and will do a Proof of Concept as needed.

Step 3: Design

Concept proposal is evaluated and a final Design will come out from this step.

Step 4: Plan

Project Plan is build a review for it's execution

Step 5: Execute

Here is where the machine is build according to the plan.

Step 6: Transition

Machine documents are put into Agile, here is where Maintenance training happen.

Step 7: Support

Automation team will provide support during the life time of the equipment.
